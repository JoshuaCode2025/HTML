Admisson Requirments for HCIT

  • A National Senior Certificate with an endorsment for admission to a higher Certificate or Diploma or Degree : or
  • A National Certificate Vocational(NVC)at NQF Level 4 within an associated field and levels of achievements or :
  • Mature Age Exemption for students above the age of 23 as set out in the higher Education Regulations: or
  • Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL)
